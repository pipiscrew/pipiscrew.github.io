Is a complete suite (IDE, compiler, programming language, image sprite level resource editor) intended for developing games/demos for the 8 / 16-bit line of computers, with a focus on the MOS 6502 & the Motorola 68000, supporting the C64, C128, VIC-20, PLUS4, NES, PET and Amiga 500.
