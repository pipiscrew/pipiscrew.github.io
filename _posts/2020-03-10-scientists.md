title: Scientists discover a pulsating star that only gets brighter in one hemisphere

date: 2020-03-10
categories: [news]
toc: true
---

Researchers at Penn State have announced they have discovered a new type of pulsating star with a brightness that oscillates periodically but only over one of its hemispheres. Pulsating stars typically have brightness that oscillates periodically across its entire surface.
