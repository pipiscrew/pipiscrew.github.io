An online-only bar giving isolated extroverts the chance to meet up over a cold beer in the comfort of their own homes has opened in St Petersburg.

Russian creative agency the Shishki Collective launched The Stay the Fuck Home bar to encourage more people to stay at home during the Covid-19 pandemic.
