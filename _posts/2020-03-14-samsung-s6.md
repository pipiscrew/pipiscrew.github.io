<span style="background-color: #ffff00;">/e/ for S6</span>

/e/ is a [fork](https://www.xda-developers.com/e-google-free-lineageos-fork-nexus-oneplus-xiaomi/) of LineageOS created by Gaël Duval that is intended to be "free from Google". It replaces Google Play Services with [MicroG](https://microg.org/), a free and open-source implementation of Google APIs.
