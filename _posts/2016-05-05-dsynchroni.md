Lightweight utility that lets you synchronize two or more folders, create inclusion and exclusion filters, schedule jobs and view logs in the main window.

Is a stand-alone utility that let you periodically synchronize two or more folders on Hard Disk, Floppy Disk, LAN, USB Key, CD-DVD (with packet writing software) and FTP server.
