The entry submission period for DV-2018 is from 12:00PM EDT (GMT -4) on **October 4, 2016** to 12:00PM EST (GMT -5) on **November 7, 2016**. The entry form will only be available for submission during this period and this period only. 

https://www.dvlottery.state.gov/
