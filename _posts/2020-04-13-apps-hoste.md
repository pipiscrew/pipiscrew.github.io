title: Apps hosted on .netlify.com to redirect to .netlify.app starting April 14, 2020
author: PipisCrew
date: 2020-04-13
categories: [news]
toc: true
---

Starting April 14, 2020, sites without a custom domain are being moved from site-name.netlify.com to site-name.netlify.app . Traffic to any site-name.netlify.com address will be forwarded to site-name.netlify.app using an HTTP 301 redirect. New websites will also have URLs ending with netlify.
