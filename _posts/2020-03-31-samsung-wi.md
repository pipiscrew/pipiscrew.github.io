title: Samsung will be ending all LCD production by the end of 2020

categories: [news]
toc: true

According to a spokeswoman, Samsung Display, which is a subunit of the South Korean tech giant Samsung Electronics, will be discontinuing all manufacturing of liquid crystal display (LCD) panels in South Korea and China by the end of this year.
