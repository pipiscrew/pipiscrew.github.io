Transcranial magnetic stimulation is a relatively new medical treatment that uses **electromagnetism** to change the brain’s neural networks. The U.S. Food and Drug Administration has approved TMS machines to treat major depressive disorder, migraines and obsessive-compulsive disorder, and the therapy is rigorously studied — with [published scientific research](https://www.researchgate.net/search/publication?q=%22transcranial+magnetic+stimulation%22) proving its safety and effectiveness.
